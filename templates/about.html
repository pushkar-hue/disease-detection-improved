About Medical AI Diagnostic Tool

Important: This application is for educational and demonstration purposes only. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice and diagnosis.

Purpose

This tool demonstrates the capabilities of artificial intelligence in medical image analysis. Using deep learning models trained on various medical imaging datasets, it can perform preliminary analysis on several types of medical images.

Models

Breast Cancer Detection

Analyzes breast histopathology images to detect cancerous tissue.

Classes: Healthy, Sick

COVID-19 Detection

Analyzes chest X-rays to identify potential COVID-19 infection.

Classes: COVID, Lung Opacity, Normal, Viral Pneumonia

Malaria Detection

Examines blood smear images to detect malarial parasites.

Classes: Parasitized, Uninfected

Pneumonia Detection

Analyzes chest X-rays to identify potential pneumonia.

Classes: Normal, Pneumonia

Skin Cancer Detection

Examines dermatoscopic images to detect potential skin cancers.

Classes: Benign, Malignant

Technology

This application is built using the following technologies:

  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Deep Learning: PyTorch with EfficientNet B0 architecture
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript with Bootstrap 5

Limitations

This tool has several important limitations:

  • It is intended for educational purposes only, not for clinical use
  • Models are trained on limited datasets and may not generalize well to all images
  • Results should always be verified by healthcare professionals
  • This is not a substitute for proper medical diagnosis and treatment
